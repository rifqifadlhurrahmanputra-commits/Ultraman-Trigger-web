The Rise of Ancient Light

Ultraman Trigger: New Generation Tiga tells the story of Kengo Manaka, a young man whose motto is “Smile, Smile!” and who lives in a time when humanity has expanded to Mars under the protection of the defense team GUTS-Select, but their peace is shattered by the return of the three Dark Giants Carmeara, Darrgon, and Hudram ancient beings from a war between light and darkness that occurred 300 million years ago. as the threat rises, Kengo discovers that he carries the power of light and transforms into Ultraman Trigger to protect the people he cares about, fighting in multiple forms such as Multi Type, Power Type, Sky Type, and the powerful Glitter Trigger Eternity, while the truth about his past connection with Carmeara gradually unfolds, leading to an emotional conflict between his personal feelings and his duty as Earth’s protector, creating a story filled with action, drama, and hope.
